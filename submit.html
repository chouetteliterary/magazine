HOW TO SUBMIT TO CHOUETTE

Submissions are currently CLOSED!

To submit to chouette, please send an email to chouetteliterary@gmail.com.

-The subject line should contain your full name and submission category (ie. Elizabeth Bennett / Fiction)

-Your document should be titled "Author Name - Title of Piece" (ie. Leo Tolstoy - Anna Karenina) and attached as a .docx file, or .pdf for pieces with special formatting

Prose submissions should be double-spaced and all submissions should be in an easy-to-read font (ie. Times New Roman or EB Garamondâ€”our personal favourite!). If you are submitting to more than one category, please send separate email submissions.
SUBMISSION CATEGORIES
FICTION
Please submit one fiction piece of no more than 2500 words. If you're submitting flash fiction, you may submit up to three pieces of no more than 2500 words total.
NON-FICTION
Please submit one non-fiction piece of no more than 2500 words. Creative non-fiction includes, but is not limited to: memoir, personal essay, journalistic essay.
POETRY
Please submit no more than three poems, to a maximum of five pages.
ART
Please submit no more than three pieces. It can be photography, digital art, or any other visual medium.
