ABOUT US
chouette is a montreal-based online literary magazine. we accept fiction, non-fiction, poetry, and visual art. we are drawn to pieces that concern themselves with the little things—the day-to-day minutiae that make our nests. you can find our submission info here!
MASTHEAD
Alyssa Sequeira (she/her)
is a recent graduate of English literature at McGill, where she is now pursuing her master's with the goal of teaching high school English. One of her literary passions lies in the haunting depths of Gothic literature, with favourites like Wuthering Heights and Rebecca, but her love for reading defies genres. She is really excited to read and curate your work with friends she loves!
Gabrielle Cole (she/her)
is a Jamaican-Canadian writer currently residing in Montreal. She holds a B.A. in English literature from McGill University, where she nurtured a love for twentieth and twenty-first century American literature (for all its lengthy and highly-punctuated asides—like this one). When she's not reading or writing, you can find her logging movies on Letterboxd.
Lauren Bullock (she/her)
is currently studying Publishing at Centennial College after completing her B.A. in English Literature at McGill University. She enjoys reading science fiction, contemporary AAPI literature, and any book that will make her cry. As Lauren is prone to reading slumps, she also finds comfort in horror movies and moscow mules.
Suzanna Graham (she/her)
is a girl with a lit degree and a fascination in medieval literature. She's currently working in Montreal, attempting to learn french, and skating on frozen lakes. In her spare time she likes to bake loafs, run around, and watch the latest Netflix docuseries.
